Mandate & origin

Founded where capital meets Africa's green economy

At the convergence of private capital flows, tightening ESG expectations, and a persistent absence of credible on-the-ground advisory capacity. The firm exists to close that gap with rigour, not rhetoric.

01
Positioning

Neither local nor at arm's length

Principal-led, lean by design, internationally credible by formation. Neither a local firm scaling globally, nor a Western firm at arm's length — built for markets that reward proximity and penalise detachment.

02

Advisory Services for Complex Markets

Every investment context is unique. Vertimpact combines regional expertise, institutional insight, and execution capability to support international investors across African markets — from initial strategy through to long-term value creation.

Market Entry & Investment Advisory

We support international investors, funds, and corporations entering African markets through rigorous market analysis, partner identification, regulatory navigation, and end-to-end execution support.

ESG & Sustainability Advisory

We help organisations strengthen ESG positioning and align with global standards, delivering sustainability strategies, compliance frameworks, and reporting aligned with investor and stakeholder expectations.

Impact Investment & DFI Advisory

We provide strategic advisory to impact investors and development finance institutions, including impact measurement design, blended finance structuring, and pipeline development across priority sectors.

Clean Energy & Carbon Projects

We support clean energy and carbon market initiatives through market intelligence, project development advisory, and strategic guidance across voluntary carbon markets and nature-based solutions.
